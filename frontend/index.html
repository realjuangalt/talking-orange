Bitcoin AR Experience

Welcome to the Future of Bitcoin Education!

This AR experience brings Bitcoin to life through an interactive 3D orange character that can talk, listen, and answer your questions about Bitcoin.

How to use:

  1. Print the talking orange marker
  2. Point your camera at the printed image
  3. Watch the 3D orange character come to life!
  4. Use the microphone to talk to the character
